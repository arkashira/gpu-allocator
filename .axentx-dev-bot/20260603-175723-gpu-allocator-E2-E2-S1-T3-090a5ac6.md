* What could go wrong: Policy violations may not be detected in real-time due to technical issues or misconfiguration.
* How to detect: Monitor the alert system for failed or delayed alerts, and investigate any issues that arise.
* What could go wrong: Alerts may be sent to the wrong recipients or with incorrect information.
* How to detect: Verify that alerts are being sent to the correct recipients and contain accurate information.
* What could go wrong: The alert system may be overwhelmed by a large number of policy violations, leading to delays or failures.
* How to detect: Monitor the alert system for performance issues, and implement load balancing or other measures to prevent overload.
